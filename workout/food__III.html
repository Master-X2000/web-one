Eggs

Eggs are the perfect post-workout food because they are high in protein and are also a great source of many other nutrients, which help your body repair after an intense gym session. You can have eggs in whichever way you want to, be it scrambled, poached, boiled or a sunny side up. Discarding the yolk and having only the egg whites is also a good choice as egg whites contain no fat or cholesterol. The best way to have eggs after your workout is to have five egg whites and one whole egg mixed - to extract the maximum benefits of the egg whites as well as the yolk of one egg.
