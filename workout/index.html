It is always recommended that you consume a meal rich in carbs and proteins immediately after you have finished exercising. Most fitness experts suggest that you eat your meal within a period of 45 minutes of exercising. Keep in mind that post a workout session, you should not remain without food beyond 2 hours. Following a good, nutritious diet post-workout will help you go a long way in achieving your fitness goals.
